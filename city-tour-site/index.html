Top three activities to do at Vancouver

Attend a show or a concert

The Queen Elizabeth Theatre is a great arts venue to spend your night out.

Go for a hiking at Cypress mountain

Cypress mountain has trails with various difficulties, getting some sweat, or a chill walk.

Grab a meal in Richmond

Have some dim sum as brunch, hot pot for dinner, end your day with bubble tea.
